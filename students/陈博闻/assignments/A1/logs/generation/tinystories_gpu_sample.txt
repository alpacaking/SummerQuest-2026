Once upon a time, in a small town, there was a little boy named Tim. Tim had a big box of toys. He loved to play with his toys every day. One day, Tim found a new toy in his box. It was a shiny diamond. Tim was very happy.
Tim showed the diamond to his friend, Sam. Sam said, "Wow, that's a cool diamond! Can I play with it?" Tim said, "Yes, you can play with it, but you must be careful." Sam played with the diamond and it went "zoom, go high up!" They had lots of fun with the diamond.
But then, something unexpected happened. A big wind came and blew the diamond out of Tim's hands. The diamond flew up into the sky and landed in a tree. Tim and Sam were sad. They looked at the diamond and saw that it was gone. They looked and looked, but they could not find it. The diamond was gone, and they were very sad.
